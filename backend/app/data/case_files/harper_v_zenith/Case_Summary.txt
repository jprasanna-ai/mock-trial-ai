HARPER v. ZENITH PHARMACEUTICALS
Civil Case No. 2024-CV-3892

CASE OVERVIEW
=============

Riley Harper brings this products liability action against Zenith Pharmaceuticals, 
Inc., alleging that the company's medication "Neurocalm" caused severe neurological 
side effects that have permanently impaired the plaintiff's quality of life.

CAUSE OF ACTION
---------------
Products Liability - Failure to Warn
Negligence
Breach of Implied Warranty

DAMAGES SOUGHT
--------------
- Past and future medical expenses
- Lost wages and earning capacity  
- Pain and suffering
- Punitive damages
