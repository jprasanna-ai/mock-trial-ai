STIPULATIONS
HARPER v. ZENITH PHARMACEUTICALS, INC.
Case No. 2024-CV-3892

The parties hereby stipulate and agree to the following facts:

1. Riley Harper was prescribed Neurocalm by Dr. Sarah Mitchell on May 15, 2023, 
   for treatment of generalized anxiety disorder.

2. Riley Harper took Neurocalm as prescribed from May 15, 2023, through 
   August 20, 2023.

3. Zenith Pharmaceuticals, Inc. is the manufacturer and distributor of Neurocalm.

4. Neurocalm received FDA approval on January 12, 2022.

5. The FDA-approved package insert for Neurocalm that was in effect during the 
   relevant time period is authentic and may be admitted as Exhibit 1.

6. Riley Harper's medical records are authentic and may be admitted as Exhibit 2.

7. All expert witnesses are qualified to testify in their respective areas of 
   expertise.

8. The chain of custody for all physical evidence has been properly maintained.

9. Zenith Pharmaceuticals' annual revenue for 2023 was $4.2 billion.

10. Neurocalm accounted for approximately $380 million in sales in 2023.
