STATE OF MIDLANDS v. AVERY
Criminal Case No. 2024-CR-1847

CASE OVERVIEW
=============

The State of Midlands has charged Jordan Avery with Second Degree Murder in the death of 
Taylor Morgan. The incident occurred on January 15, 2024, at the Riverside Community Center
during a charity event.

KEY FACTS
---------
- Taylor Morgan died from blunt force trauma to the head
- Jordan Avery was present at the scene
- The two had a known business dispute
- Multiple witnesses observed interactions between the parties

CHARGE
------
Second Degree Murder (Midlands Penal Code § 187.2)
"The unlawful killing of a human being with malice aforethought, but without premeditation."
