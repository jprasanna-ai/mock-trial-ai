EXTERNAL EXAMINATION
====================
The body is that of a well-developed, well-nourished adult measuring 5'10" and 
weighing approximately 175 pounds. Rigor mortis is fully developed. Livor mortis 
is fixed and posterior.

INJURIES
========
1. Contusion to the right temporal region measuring 3.5 cm x 2.0 cm
2. Underlying depressed skull fracture of the temporal bone
3. Epidural hematoma, right side, approximately 150 ml
4. Associated cerebral contusion

CAUSE OF DEATH
==============
Blunt force trauma to the head resulting in skull fracture and epidural hematoma.

MANNER OF DEATH
===============
Homicide

TOXICOLOGY
==========
Blood alcohol content: 0.04%
No controlled substances detected

OPINION
=======
Based on my examination, the decedent died as a result of blunt force trauma to 
the right side of the head. The injury pattern is consistent with a single, 
forceful blow from a blunt object. The location and severity of the injury 
indicates significant force was applied.

The injury could be consistent with:
- Impact from a heavy blunt instrument
- Forceful contact with a hard surface (such as a fall onto concrete)

Time of death is estimated between 9:00 PM and 9:45 PM on January 15, 2024.
