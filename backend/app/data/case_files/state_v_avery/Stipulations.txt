STIPULATIONS
STATE OF MIDLANDS v. JORDAN AVERY
Case No. 2024-CR-1847

The parties hereby stipulate and agree to the following facts:

1. Taylor Morgan died on January 15, 2024, at the Riverside Community Center.

2. The cause of death was blunt force trauma to the head, as determined by the 
   Midlands County Medical Examiner.

3. Jordan Avery was present at the Riverside Community Center on the evening 
   of January 15, 2024.

4. Jordan Avery and Taylor Morgan had a prior business relationship through 
   Avery-Morgan Consulting, LLC.

5. The charity event at the Riverside Community Center was attended by 
   approximately 150 guests.

6. Security camera footage from Camera #7 accurately depicts events in the 
   main hall between 9:00 PM and 10:00 PM on the date in question.

7. All exhibits have been properly authenticated and may be admitted without 
   further foundation.

8. All witnesses are competent to testify.

9. Chain of custody for all physical evidence has been properly maintained.

10. The defendant was read Miranda rights at 10:15 PM on January 15, 2024, 
    and voluntarily waived those rights before speaking with police.
